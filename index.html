Frank Laiga.

Musician / Producer.

Bio

Frank is a Kenyan-based producer/artist behind the best covers on YouTube such as Magix - All Over. He’s also produced music of his own such as Thiha which can be found on YouTube. He is doing exactly what he wants and the result is some of the most compelling music of his career.

Frank Laiga is strictly a solo project, allowing him to have complete control over his creative vision. While it is easy to identify his foundational reverence for pop/afro, Frank creates is not quite like anything else – he infuses a patchwork of musical styles and incorporates production elements from genres as diverse as modern pop to afro beats. There are big melodic hooks, sampled beats, funk inspired guitar takes, and harmony laden vocals that reveal an affection for afro and pop. These seemingly disparate elements combine to create a truly compelling listen.

Frank made official debut in 2017 with the full length ‘Thiha.’ A little bit eccentric but still supremely accessible, it serves as a perfect introduction to new listeners. While the album probably won’t be topping the charts any time soon, it is filled with enough open hearted melodies and experimental flourishes to capture the attention of a certain brand of music fan, and in the process, establish Frank as a true original.
